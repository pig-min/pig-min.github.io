New Freeware game [Sugar Cube] released, by [Cut & Paste] maker Turtle Cream!
A cube of sugar escapes the factory to avoid the fate of being a cookie!

Seoul, South Korea, Aug. 24th. 2010 - Korean Indie game team Turtle Cream released 2nd. Freeware game [Sugar Cube].

[Sugar Cube] is a 2D platformer game that presents the story of a sugar cube. The backgrounds of the game have two sides, namely, the front and the back. So depending on how you play, the environments will very often be vastly different.

This game includes fifteen levels, with the cookie factory, the chocolate factory, and the candy factory as the backdrops. It also offers eleven achievements and a level editor, and Chip-Tune style music soundtrack.

More information (Screenshot / Youtube / Game download) : http://luvtext.net/tc/eng/16

PR contact : mrkwang@pig-min.com (Pig-Min Agency)

About Turtle Cream:
"Tuttle Cream" is a studio founded around students of the Sogang Univ. Game education center in Korea. It strives to create small but unique games. The team showed its potential with its first production, "Cut & Paste," which was also highlighted by various media including GameSpot and GameSetWatch.

About Pig-Min Agency :
Pig-Min was 4-years-old Indie Game blog webzine for Korean, and decided to support Korean Indie Games to go abroad from 1 year ago, under the name of Pig-Min Agency. [Sand castle - Prelude : The Faded Memories] is 2nd. work by them, and 1st. work was [Cut & Paste] by Turtle Cream.
