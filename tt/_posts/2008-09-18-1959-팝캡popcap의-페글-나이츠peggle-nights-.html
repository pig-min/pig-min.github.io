--- layout: post title: "팝캡(Popcap)의 [페글 나이츠(Peggle Nights)] 발매!" date: "2008-09-18 01:25:46+09:00" categories: "뉴스" redirect_from: /tt/1959 --- 며칠 전 뉴스에서는 [페글 나이츠(Peggle Nights)]의 발매가 며칠 미뤄질지도 모른다고 했습니다만, 어제부로 [페글 나이츠]가 발매되고 말았습니다.

시대의 유행(?)인 '트로피 룸'.

현재로써는 팝캡에서만 판매하는 듯 싶지만, 조만간 스팀(Steam)이나 빅 피쉬 게임즈(Big Fish Games) 같은 곳에 다 들어가겠죠.

아직은 플레이 초반이라 뭐라 평가하기 힘들고, 조만간 프리뷰 / 리뷰를 올리도록 하겠습니다.

아래는 [페글 나이츠]에서 새롭게 등장한 부분을 보도자료에서 긁어왔습니다. 해석은 첫 단어만 하도록 하죠.

? 신 캐릭터와 파워 업(New Peggle Master/power-up) : “Marina,” the electric squid, brings the number of Peggle Masters / power-ups to 11; her “Shockwave” effect arcs between the first peg hit and the bucket at the bottom, clearing all intervening pegs with a jolt of electricity

? 새로운 트로피와 아치브먼트(New Trophy/Achievement) : In addition to the original game’s “all clear” achievement, Peggle Nights sets an “ace” score for each level; “acing” each level earns a special trophy

? 더 많은 트로피들(More Trophies) : The new game offers four different trophies for the various achievements: completing Adventure Mode, completing Challenge Mode, clearing all levels of all pegs, and “acing” all levels. Oh, and a new trophy room to keep ’em in!

? 60개의 새롭게 디자인 된 레벨들(60 Newly Designed Levels) : Each Peggle Master has five themed “dream state” levels in the game’s main Adventure mode, plus a final five Master levels in which the player chooses the Master/power-up

? 60개의 새로운 도전 과제들(60 New Challenges) : Five dozen new Challenge Mode levels, with many fiendishly tricky objectives for players to reach; new types of challenges include “low ball” challenges in which the player tries to get the lowest possible score, Style Shot based challenges and challenges involving specific or combined power-ups

? 새로운 스타일 샷(New Style Shots) : More ways to score bonus points based on the player’s aim and timing acumen: “Off the Wall,” “Double Long Shot” and more

? 전부 새롭게 만든 음악 사운드트랙(All-New Soundtrack) : Maintaining the cultured sensibilities of the original Peggle, the new game offers a mix of original tunes and well-known classical music ? including a fully-orchestrated introduction by the Slovak National Symphony Orchestra

? 당신의 스킬을 존경하라 - 드랙 & 드롭으로 새 창을 열어 리플레이를 볼 수 있음(Admire Your Skillz) : You can now drag and drop replays of your top shots into the open game window to view them

? 파워-업의 개선(Power-Up Improvements): Existing power-ups including Tula’s “flower power,” Kat Tut’s “pyramid” and Lord Cinderbottom’s “fireball” have been tweaked to optimize their effectiveness

? 다운로드 받을 수 있는 추가 레벨 팩(Level Packs) (Coming Soon): Peggle Nights is designed to accept additional levels and PopCap plans to begin issuing custom-made, in some cases themed, Level Packs

P.S. : 과연 [페글 나이츠]는 스팀의 '달성 목표'에 연동을 할 것인가 안 할 것인가?
