Moon lights Green at Steam!

'6180 the moon' is on Steam Greenlight, by the developer of 'Sugar Cube: Bittersweet Factory'.

Seoul, South Korea, Jan. 23th. 2013 - Korean Indie team Turtle Cream started Steam Greenlight campaign of upcoming game '6180 the Moon'.

'6180 the moon' is platformer game with unique mechanic. "Moon jumps very high". Top and Bottom of the screen are connected. Gamer doesn't die, even if moon fell down. It will be released at Feb. 2013.

'6180 the moon' is 2nd. commercial game by Turtle Cream. 1st. commercial game was Sweetest Platformer, 'Sugar Cube: Bittersweet Factory' at Steam.

* Please upvote at Steam Greenlight, to let this bright 'Moon' enter Steam.
http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=121952034

About Turtle Cream:
"Tuttle Cream" started career as students of the Sogang Univ. Game education center in Korea. From 2011, they became independent studio. Their 1st. freeware 'Cut & Paste' got several positive feedbacks, 2nd. freeware 'Sugar Cube' won IGF 2010 China Best Game. 1st. commercial game 'Sugar Cube: Bittersweet Factory' is available at Steam. They are making 2nd. commercial game '6180 the Moon' now.
http://turtle-cream.com
