'The Sweet Song' M/V released for Sugar-Free game!

Korean famous indie pop singer 'Yeongene' recorded sweet theme music of 'Sugar Cube: Bittersweet Factory'.

Seoul, South Korea, Mar. 1st. 2011 - Korean Indie team Turtle Cream released Music Video of 'The Sweet Song', main theme song of recent release 'Sugar Cube: Bittersweet Factory'.

'Yeongene' is Korean indie pop singer, who is famous for the band 'Linus' Blanket'. She also did collabroation 'Save our smile' with BMX Bandits.

"We hoped 'Yeongene' to sing this song, when we made early version of this song." says Sun Park, CEO of Turtle Cream. "I was big fan of her work in the past. We were not sure to work with her, because she is too great. As we tried to contact her, she agreed to record this song. Now she recorded song for our game. We really appreciate her, as a fan & indie game maker!"

'The Sweet Song' M/V is available at Youtube - Vimeo. Plus we uploaded to 'The Sweet Song' single at bandcamp. Full length soundtrack of 'SC:BF' will be released next week at Bandcamp.


* About Game

'Sugar Cube : Bittersweet Factory' is commercial version of 'Sugar Cube', which won 2010 IGF China best game. It is a 2D platformer game that presents the story of a sugar cube. The backgrounds of the game have two sides, namely, the front and the back. So depending on how you play, the environments will very often be vastly different.

It has 5 world themes containing unique 90 levels, 20 achievements, and 2 different endings. Plus it has SUPER CUTE looks and very sweet soundtrack.

You could purchase it through several digital distribution stores for Windows PC, as Desura - Indiecity - Indievania - Direct purchase through official site. It will be available at some local digital stores as Kawagames(Spain) - Playism(Japan) in the near future.
