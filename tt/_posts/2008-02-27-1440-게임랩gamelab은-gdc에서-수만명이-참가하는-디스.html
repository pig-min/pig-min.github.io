--- layout: post title: "게임랩(Gamelab)은 GDC에서, 수만명(!)이 참가하는 [디스트로이 올 디벨롭퍼스(Destroy all Developers)]를 했다!" date: "2008-02-27 07:00:44+09:00" categories: "뉴스" redirect_from: /tt/1440 --- 게임랩(Gamelab)에서는 매년, 컨퍼런스동안 참가자들이 참여할 수 있는 소셜 게임(Social Game)을 만들고 있습니다. 2년 전 부터는 행사 주최측인 CMP에서도 약간의 후원을 받게 되었고요. 이번 GDC 2008에서는, [디스트로이 올 디벨롭퍼스(Destroy all Developers)]라는 게임을 했다고 합니다!

개인적으로 이 게임에 대한 소식을 지난주부터 들어왔지만, 어떻게 플레이하는 것인지 감이 전혀 오지 않아(...), 묵혀두었다가 지금 올립니다. 해보지 않아 감이 오지 않으니, Pig-Min 정모용(...)으로 이식해달라고 부탁이라도 해야되는게 아닐까도 싶음.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

HOW IT WORKS:

COLLECT BUSINESS CARDS to complete mission cards, which you can find at game HQ on the second floor of Moscone North or in your conference bag.

JOIN A TEAM - art, code, biz, production, audio, or game design. And settle your final scores with your fellow developers, once and for all.

PLAY ALL DAY. Game HQ is open Tues-Thurs, 9am-6pm. But you can collect business cards and scheme with your teammates anytime.

LEVEL UP by completing new missions. Earn GAMER and HARDCORE badge ribbons, Destroy All Developers t-shirts, and other game swag.

BECOME AN ASSASSIN when you reach HARDCORE level and collect the business cards of particular players for extra points.

WIN BIG when the game ends Thursday at 7:30. Stop by Game HQ on the 2nd Floor of Mascone West to claim your victory. Prizes will be given to top scoring players - plus a prize drawing from members of the winning teams. The more missions you complete, the better your chances.
