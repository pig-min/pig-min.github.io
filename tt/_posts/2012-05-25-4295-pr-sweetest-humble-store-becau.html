Sweetest Humble Store & Because we may!

2 good news about 'Sugar Cube: Bittersweet Factory', Humble Store & Because we may sale.

Seoul, South Korea, May. 24. 2012 - Korean Indie team Turtle Cream got 2 good news for their game 'Sugar Cube: Bittersweet Factory'.

First of all, Humble Store is available for Turtle Cream. 'Humble Store' is store tool from 'Humble Indie Bundle'. It's in alpha, not available for public use yet. However Turtle Cream got permission to use it. Sun Park, CEO of Turtle Cream is really excited with that. "I liked how Humble works. I wished to add Humble Store cart to our homepage, because it looks beautiful and awesome. Now one of my dream came true!"

Right after starting Humble Store, Turtle Cream joined 'Because we may' promotion. 'Because we may' is group discount promotion run by Ron Carmel(2dboy) about 'Freedom to price'. Turtle Cream submitted 'Sugar Cube: Bittersweet Factory' as 1.49$, 70% discount, from May 24 through June 1.

Even 'Boing Boing' and 'Play This Thing' liked this game, because it's sweetest platformer ever. Now it's your turn to check why they liked this game. Don't miss this 70% discount chance!

* About Game

'Sugar Cube : Bittersweet Factory' is commercial version of 'Sugar Cube', which won 2010 IGF China best game. It is a 2D platformer game that presents the story of a sugar cube. The backgrounds of the game have two sides, namely, the front and the back. So depending on how you play, the environments will very often be vastly different.

It has 5 world themes containing unique 90 levels, 20 achievements, and 2 different endings. Plus it has SUPER CUTE looks and very sweet soundtrack.

You could purchase it through several digital distribution stores for Windows PC, as Desura - Indievania - Direct purchase(Humble Store) through official site. It will be available at some local digital stores as Kawagames(Spain) - Playism(Japan) in the near future.
