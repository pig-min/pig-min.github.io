About the Deal -- Name Your Price!
Funded by a successful Kickstarter campaign in February 2012, Guns of Icarus Online is a multiplayer combat game set in a stunning steampunk world. Raising the stakes on team-based PvP combat with an emphasis on strategic competition and truly cooperative play, Guns of Icarus Online is all about teamwork, tactics, and fast-paced action. Captain or serve as crew aboard an airship with your friends and fly into battle. With a good ship and the right crew, you can conquer the skies!
 
For a minimum of $1, gamers can name their price for Guns of Icarus Online to receive a Steam key and get access to same multiplayer experience that retails for $19.99. Customers who beat the average also get Collectors' Edition DLC and an MP3 soundtrack (a $33 value). Developer Muse Games has selected The David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust (https://www.sheldrickwildlifetrust.org/) to receive 10% of the proceeds.
 
About IndieGameStand
Since its September 26 debut, IndieGameStand has featured a new game every four days -- it's a win-win for developers trying to grow their audience AND for gamers in search of a good bargain!
