요령은 다음과 같음.

- [갈콘] 티셔츠 디자인을 만든다.
- 디자인을 이메일로 [갈콘] 제작자에게 보내고, [갈콘] 포럼에 올린다. (올리지 않으면 제작자가 대신 올림.)
- 제작자가 팔고 싶어하는 디자인을 뽑는다!
- 당선된 분은 [갈콘] 티셔츠를 받고, 또한 [갈콘] 12개들이 라이센스 팩을 받는다!
- 그 외 선정된 분들도 [갈콘] 라이센스를 받는다!

- 공모 시작 : 8월 1일
- 공모 끝 : 8월 31일
- 한 사람이 여러 개 디자인을 응모해도 됨.
- 한 사람이 여러 디자인을 응모했을 때, 상을 여러 개 받을 수도 있음.

그 외에도 여러가지 얘기가 많지만 다 해석하기는 좀 엄하니, 영어 원문은 아래 감춰두겠습니다.

[갈콘] 티셔츠 응모에 관한 영어 원문 클릭해서 읽기. 다 읽었으니 닫기.
Hey,

Well, I think it's about time that we had some Galcon t-shirts, 'eh?  Here's the deal:

- You design a Galcon t-shirt! 
- E-mail me your design, and optionally post it to the forums to show off!  (If you don't post it, I might!)
- I select the ones I want to sell!
- If you made one of the ones I sell, I'll send you one of 'em for free!  You'll also receive a Galcon license 12-pack!
- All honorable mentions will receive a Galcon license!

Design parameters:
- Max 10"x10" - may have front, back, or both.
- 200 DPI
- PNG output - with transparency where you want the black t-shirt to show-through.

Tips:
- Press F4 during the game to take a screenshot!
- Make sure your designs and slogans are "family friendly"
- Feel free to discuss potential slogans in the forums.
- Feel free to share design prototypes in the forums to get feedback from other users (you should scale your image down and post a ~500x500 .JPG for the forums.)
- Feel free to use the Galcon logo and artwork from:
http://www.imitationpickles.org/galcon/press.html
- Here's a *huge* version of the Galcon logo:
http://www.imitationpickles.org/galcon/content/bwlogo.png
- Here's the in-game font is "Zeroes Three" trademark of Ray Larabie:
http://www.myfonts.com/fonts/larabie/zeroes/
- Have fun!

Rules:
- Competition starts: August 1st.
- Competition ends: August 31st.
- You may submit as many designs as you like.
- You may win more than one prize!  (If I choose all 10 of your designs, you'll get 10 t-shirts and 120 Galcon licenses!*)

* Can you even IMAGINE anything better??!

Legal blah blah:
- When you send me your design you will be giving me copyright of that design and any slogans in the design.
- For the purpose of making these designs, you may use Galcon screenshots, the Galcon logo and other Galcon artwork.
- http://www.larabiefonts.com/help.html grants us permission to use the font for artwork.

Good Luck!
- Phil
